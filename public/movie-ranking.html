My top 3 films of all time

Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes back

Almost the perfect sequel film, contains great plot developement where all elements fit neatly and also includes epic and meaningful action scenes.

It's a Wonderful Life

Excellent Christmas classic, great feel-good story full of hope that the whole family can enjoy.

The Wizard of Oz

Classic epic all film fans should see for it's great historical achievements and effect on culture.
